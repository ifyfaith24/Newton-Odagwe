My Work

CORE BANKING APPLICATION

This is a retail core banking application designed to manage and automate the daily operations of retail banking services. It is the backbone of banking operations, enabling financial institutions to deliver efficient, secure, and customer-centric service. It is a micro-service-based application. I assisted in leading the development team to envision and develop some of the micro-services such as User and role management, Currency, Segregation of duties, Chart of Accounts and Fiscal Period. It is a highly permission-based system envisioned in such a way as to limit fraud in the system.

Easy Cash (9-5) Loan Application

This monolithic loan application is targeted at working-class citizens within a geographical location. It comprises both the user interface and the admin web application. This solution aims to help working-class citizens access loans by applying for loans to help with financial needs until their salary is paid. Little interest is paid in the process, and the bank uses the method to source new customers and increase its customer base.

Bank Quick Service Portal

QSP is a digital platform designed to streamline and enhance the efficiency of service delivery for businesses and their customers. It provides an intuitive interface that enables users to perform essential tasks quickly and conveniently. This is a microservice architecture and the aim is to reduce the inflow of customers into the bank's branches for issues resolution and complaints. Customers can serve themselves through these channels and feedback is sent to them promptly and efficiently.
